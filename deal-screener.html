How to Estimate ARV

Step 1 — Find comps
Search Zillow/Redfin for sold homes within 0.5 miles, same bed/bath count, sold in last 90 days. Fully updated condition only.
Step 2 — Price per sq ft
Take 3–5 comps, calculate $/sqft for each. Average them. Multiply by your subject property's sqft.
Step 3 — Adjust conservatively
Subtract 5–10% from your calculated ARV. New investors consistently overshoot ARV — be conservative and protect your margin.
